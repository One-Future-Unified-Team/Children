Contact

Wanted to contact One Future Unified Team? The team are currently providing multiple ways for everyone to contact the team. Please contact us for important stuff like support, feedback, suggestion and bugs report and not non-important content like spam. We are an small team so we might be busy causing some late reply from the team so please be patient. We also only reply during our official business hours. For minor (7-12 ages) only their parents are allowed to contact us directly due to the services terms that we used. Parents could only help their children to contact us directly. Currently, FB do not allowed minot to register an account on their platform too so it is not possible for a minor to contact us directly.
